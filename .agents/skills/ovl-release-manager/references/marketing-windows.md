# Marketing Windows

Platform pitching windows and promotional timing guidance. Used by the
`ovl-release-manager` skill when calculating milestone dates.

## Platform Pitch Windows

### Spotify for Artists — Editorial Pitching

- **Window:** Submit between T−21 and T−7 (at least 7 days before release)
- **Recommended:** T−21 for best consideration window
- **Requirements:** Release must be uploaded to distributor and live in Spotify
  for Artists dashboard before submitting pitch
- **What to pitch:** One unreleased track per release; include mood, genre,
  instrumentation, and cultural moment
- **Notes:** Pitching does not guarantee placement; editorial playlists are
  competitive. Algorithmic playlists (Release Radar, Discover Weekly) do not
  require pitching — they respond to listener behaviour

### Apple Music for Artists

- **Window:** Verify metadata at T−14 minimum
- **Requirements:** Log in to Apple Music for Artists; confirm release appears
  and metadata is correct
- **Notes:** Apple Music does not have a public curator pitch form equivalent
  to Spotify; outreach is through Apple Music for Artists Connect or direct
  label/distributor relationships

### YouTube Music

- **Notes:** Automatically distributed when a release goes live via most
  distributors; no separate pitch process for independent artists
- **Opportunity:** YouTube Content ID registration via distributor protects
  against unlicensed use and captures ad revenue

### Bandcamp

- **Notes:** No pitch process; releases go live immediately on upload.
  Bandcamp Friday promotion (first Friday of most months) is a good release
  target — fans browse actively and Bandcamp waives its revenue share

### Independent Playlist Curators

- **Outreach window:** T−21 to T−14 for initial contact
- **Format:** Personalised message, one track, brief context on the artist
  and release. Never mass-blast; curators value individual attention.
- **Follow-up:** One follow-up only if no response after 7 days
- **Tracking:** Record outreach in `workspace/outreach/` opportunity records

## Promotional Timeline Guidance

| Activity | Timing | Notes |
| -------- | ------ | ----- |
| Pre-save / pre-add links live | T − 14 days | Generate via distributor dashboard |
| Teaser content (clip, artwork reveal) | T − 14 days | Short-form video performs well |
| Pre-save push to existing audience | T − 7 days | Email list, social posts |
| Release day "Out Now" posts | T − 0 | All active platforms simultaneously |
| Behind-the-scenes / making-of | T + 3 days | Extends release week engagement |
| Streaming milestone posts | As they happen | 100 streams, 1,000, etc. |

## Content Creator / Sync Outreach

For licensing opportunities (podcasts, video, games, film):

- **Best timing:** T−28 to T−14 if release is relevant to their content
- **Alternatively:** Evergreen outreach independent of a release window —
  most CC-licensed music is discovered and licensed long after release
- **Handled by:** Licensing Outreach agent, not the Release Manager
